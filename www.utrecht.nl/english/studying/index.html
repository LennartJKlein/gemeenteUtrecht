The Anglo-Saxon monk Willibrord, the first bishop of Utrecht, established a training centre for priests and young noblemen in 695 A.D. This seminary was later to become Utrecht University.
Today, with over 70,000 students, this is the largest and most prestigious university in the Netherlands. Utrecht is the most important education centre in our country. The city offers all the schools and educational institutions necessary for 'life-long learning'.

Useful Links

  • Utrecht University
    Utrecht University has an excellent international reputation as a research university. Its ambition is to remain one of the best universities in Europe.
  • University College Utrecht
    These pages will provide you with information about University College, the international undergraduate campus of Utrecht University.
  • Hogeschool Utrecht University of Applied Sciences
    The Hogeschool Utrecht (HU) is a university of applied sciences. Graduates from HU University are fully qualified to practise their profession without further study, licensing or registration with a professional association.
  • HKU Utrecht School of the Arts
    With around 3,500 students, the Utrecht School of the Arts is one of the largest art and culture-oriented universities of applied sciences in Europe.
  • TiasNimbas Business School
    TiasNimbas offers innovative, international management programmes and the unique opportunity to study for British and international MBA and Master’s degrees.
  • The Marnix Academie
    The Marnix Academie is a specialist university of professional education which focuses on teacher training for primary education.
  • Utrecht Summer School
    The Utrecht Summer School was founded in 1987, with one course: Dutch Culture and Society. Over the last two decades, the summer school's range of courses has developed to include over 60 courses in virtually all the disciplines offered by Utrecht University.
  • International School Utrecht
    International School Utrecht offers primary as well as secondary education. ISUtrecht teaches the world renowned International Baccalaureate Primary Years Programme (PYP) and Middle Years Programme (MYP). From 2016 the school will also offer the Diplome Programme (DP).
  • Utrecht Public Library
    Locations, opening hours, fees, E-mail addresses and so on.
  • iStip, International Student information Platform (Facebook)
    Imagine a world where all the information for students is combined for those who wants to study in a foreign country. Where information comes to you without being linked. Just one world, one platform, one easy way to use.
