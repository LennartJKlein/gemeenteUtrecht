Climb to the top of the Dom Tower and you will see that many new building projects are taking place around the central station, at the Science Park and in Leidsche Rijn, which is the biggest new residential development in the Netherlands. There may be a recession going on, but Utrecht is still doing fine! In fact, the Utrecht region has one of the most favourable expectations for economic growth of all the city regions in Western Europe thanks to its knowledge-based economy and young, highly educated population.

Utrecht, a meeting point for companies

Utrecht is the fourth largest city in The Netherlands. "Large" because of the number of residents, but also due to its important central location in the country and its boundless ambition. Hidden behind Utrecht's well-known picturesque and intimate atmosphere, is the most innovative, talent-rich and creative city in The Netherlands.
